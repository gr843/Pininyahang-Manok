PININYAHANG MANOK- FILIPINO FOOD - EASY RECIPE

HISTORY ABOUT PININYAHANG MANOK

Pininyahang Manok, or Filipino pineapple chicken, is a, creamy, sweet-and-savory stew originating from Southern Luzon, Philippines. Historically rooted in areas with high pineapple production during the Spanish colonial era, this dish features chicken simmered in coconut milk (or evaporated milk) and pineapple juice, often mixed with potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 can (20 oz) pineapple chunks, with juice reserved
  • 1 cup evaporated milk or coconut milk (gata)
  • 2-3 potatoes, cubed (optional)
  • 1 carrot, sliced (optional)
  • 1 large red/green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce (patis) or to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth or water
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

STEPS AND PROCEDURES

  • Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with the reserved pineapple juice. Let it sit for 20-30 minutes.
  • Fry Veggies (Optional): Heat oil in a pan. Fry the potatoes and carrots until they start to brown. Remove and set aside.
  • Brown the Chicken: In the same pan, heat more oil if needed. Saut√© the garlic and onion until fragrant. Add the chicken pieces (drained from the marinade, but keep the juice) and cook until they turn light brown on all sides.
  • Add Seasoning & Juice: Add the fish sauce (patis) and ground black pepper. Stir to mix. Pour in the reserved pineapple juice and chicken broth (or water). Bring to a boil.
  • Simmer: Cover the pot and lower the heat. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, or until the chicken is tender.
  • Add Veggies & Fruit: Add the potatoes, carrots, and pineapple chunks. Simmer for another 5-10 minutes until the vegetables are cooked.
  • Make it Creamy: Pour in the evaporated milk or coconut milk and add the bell peppers. Stir well and simmer uncovered for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Final Adjustment & Serve: Adjust seasoning with salt if necessary. Remove from heat and serve hot with rice.
